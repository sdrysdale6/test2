<section id="capstone">
    <h2>Capstone Reflection</h2>
    <p>
        Reflecting on my Capstone experience has provided me with profound insights into both my professional capabilities and areas for future growth. The most significant learning I've taken from this experience centers around the critical importance of systems thinking and applied expertise in healthcare. As I navigated the stages of the project—from the initial planning phase, beginning data collection through questionnaires to developing and implementing a centralized booking system for non-urgent transportation—I came to appreciate the complex nature of healthcare systems and the necessity of a holistic approach in problem-solving.
    </p>

<section id="transportation">
    <h2>Transportation Resources</h2>
    <p>
        Various resources and standard work instructions have been developed to ensure efficient and timely patient transportation. These resources include booking forms, guidelines, and standard work templates for managing non-urgent and urgent patient transportation.
    </p>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Transportation Booking Form:</strong> A form used to book patient transportation efficiently.</li>
        <li><strong>Non-Urgent Transport EMS:</strong> Standard work for booking EMS non-urgent transport.</li>
        <li><strong>ORNGE Transport:</strong> Guidelines for booking ORNGE transport for patient transfers.</li>
        <li><strong>Platinum Patient Transfers:</strong> Procedures for booking Platinum patient transfers.</li>
        <li><strong>MT Number Guide:</strong> Steps to obtain an MT number for patient transfers.</li>
    </ul>
</section>
